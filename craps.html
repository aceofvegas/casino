Game of Craps

Rules: You roll two dice. If the sum is 7 or 11 on the first roll, you win if you bet on pass line and lose if you bet on don't pass. If the sum is 2, 3, or 12 on the first roll, you lose if you bet on pass line and win if you bet on don't pass. If the sum is anything else, that becomes your point. You keep rolling until you either roll your point again and win if you bet on pass line and lose if you bet on don't pass, or roll a 7 and lose if you bet on pass line and win if you bet on don't pass.
